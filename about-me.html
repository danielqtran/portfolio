About Myself

Daniel is a UI/UX Designer from Portland, Oregon.

Before working as a designer, he spent several years in the social work field as a residential counselor working with at-risk youth ages 12-18, which taught him a great deal about the value of empathy and patience.

In his free time, Daniel loves to go on adventures, usually in some combination of hiking, food, and music.
