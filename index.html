Work

The Dreaming Zebra Foundation

Working with a group of fellow students, I helped redesign the website for The Dreaming Zebra Foundation, a non-profit organization whose mission is to provide art & music supplies to underprivileged children. We conducted user research to guide our design, which was focused on streamlining the user journeys for both donating and applying to become a beneficiary.

Fuzzy Connect

Connecting with others, whether for mentorship or research interviews, is difficult - and even more so when living in small towns or working in niche careers or academic fields. We developed an app to make connecting with others easy, so everyone can have that fuzzy feeling when they've found the perfect professional match.

Bubble Traveler

Traveling already involves planning for many different variables, and having to navigate countries' and states' varying pandemic-related restrictions can be one variable too many. Bubble Traveler is an app designed to integrate the constantly changing destination restrictions into the travel planning process, all in one place.
