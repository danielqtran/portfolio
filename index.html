Where Design Embraces Empathy

Hi! I'm Daniel Tran, a UX/UI Designer with experience in counseling, looking to solve problems through empathy, collaboration, and design.

Projects I've Worked On

Christa McAuliffe Academy School of Arts and Sciences
I led a website redesign project for an online private school, beginning with user research to understand our customers' needs and frustrations and progressing to the prototyping stage.
Silicon Radio
The final project in my UX/UI bootcamp was a website design for an online radio station focused on lesser known international music. My role was focused more on front-end development for this project.
Kiva Hackathon
This was a project for a hackathon to show a way Kiva, a microlending provider, could improve users' long-term engagement with the recipients of their microloans.
The Dreaming Zebra Foundation
Working with a group of fellow UX/UI students, I helped redesign the website for The Dreaming Zebra Foundation, a non-profit organization whose mission is to provide art & music supplies to underprivileged children.
